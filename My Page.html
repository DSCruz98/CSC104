D&D Crash Course

A quick guide for your first session

Dungeons & Dragons (commonly abbreviated as D&D or DnD) is a fantasy tabletop role-playing game (TTRPG) originally created and designed by Gary Gygax and Dave Arneson. The game was derived from miniature wargames but departs from traditional wargaming by allowing each player to create their own character to play instead of a military formation. These characters embark upon adventures within a fantasy setting (ususally) while a Dungeon Master acts as referee and storyteller.


D&D can be a difficult game to master but it is an easy one to start. With a basic understanding of some lore and rules you can be ready for any game. This guide will take you through these steps as well as some basic table etiquite and the creation of your very first character. Dungeons and Dragons is a constantly expanding gmae and new people are picking up the hobby every day if this guide doesnt answer all the questions you may have there are also many other amazing online resources.
